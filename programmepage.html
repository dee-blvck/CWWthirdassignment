Frequently Asked Questions

How long is CWW Tech Africa?
The duration depends on the program. We currrently have 3 cohort-based programmes.
  • CTA Cohort programmes - Duration of programme is 6 weeks
  • CTA Bootcamp - 4 weeks
  • CTA Soft skills training - 2 days
Is the program remote?
CWW Tech Africa programmes are virtual training programmes that allow all participants, regardless of location, to actively participate
How do I join a CWW Tech Africa program?
To join any of our programs, click on "Apply as a trainee" pn any of our programmes at www.cwwtechafrica.com
When will I be notified about the results of my application?
CWW Tech Africa will contact all applicants through email in due course. All successful applicants will be notified
Are CWW Tech Africa programmes open to undergraduate students?
Yes, we encourage all students to apply.
